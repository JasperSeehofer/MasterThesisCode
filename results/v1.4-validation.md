| naive | 534 | 0 | 0.8600 |
| exclude | 517 | 17 | 0.6600 |

**Notes:**
- Naive strategy includes all 534 events; zeros replaced with `np.finfo(float).tiny`.
- Exclude strategy drops 17 events that have any zero likelihood across the h-sweep.
- Physics-floor strategy uses the per-event minimum nonzero likelihood as floor for
  14 events with partial zeros; the 3 all-zero events (rows 162, 220, 499) are excluded
  (no nonzero value available to derive a physics-motivated floor).
