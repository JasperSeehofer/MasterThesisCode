# Roadmap Archive: v1.2 Production Campaign & Physics Corrections

**Archived:** 2026-04-02

## Milestone Summary

Fixed two known physics bugs in the EMRI pipeline (galactic confusion noise, Fisher matrix stencil order), validated the corrections at small scale, replaced the KDE-based detection probability with a simulation-based approach, ran a 100-task production campaign on bwUniCluster, and swept the H0 posterior over [0.6, 0.9].

**Key results:**
- LISA PSD now includes galactic foreground noise (PHYS-02 resolved)
- Fisher matrix derivatives upgraded from O(ε) forward-difference to O(ε⁴) five-point stencil (PHYS-01 resolved)
- KDE detection probability replaced by simulation-based P_det with importance sampling (VRF 11.8–24.9×)
- Production CRB catalog: 1000+ detections from 100 tasks × 50 steps (seed 200)
- Baseline MAP values: 0.72 (with BH mass), 0.86 (without); zero-likelihood problem documented
