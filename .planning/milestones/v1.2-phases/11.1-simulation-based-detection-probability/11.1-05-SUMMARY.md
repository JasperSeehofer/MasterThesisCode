## Key Results

### Injection Campaign
- **3 runs** (seed 42, 43, 44) across 7 h values: {0.60, 0.65, 0.70, 0.73, 0.80, 0.85, 0.90}
- **~290,000 total events**, 663 detections (SNR >= 15)
- z-cut importance sampling (z < 0.5) gave ~15x speedup
- HPC optimizations (batch size, GPU free interval, logging, flush) gave additional ~2.7x
- Periodic flushing (every 2000 events) prevented data loss on SLURM timeouts
- Partition: gpu_a100_short (30 min wall time)

### Evaluation
- Pipeline B evaluation ran on cpu partition (96 CPUs, ~3.5 min per h value)
- SimulationDetectionProbability loaded 379 CSVs, built P_det(d_L, M | h) grids
- P_det grids built in d_L space (no dist_to_redshift calls at query time)

### H0 Posterior Results

| Posterior | Peak h | 90% CI | h=0.73 in CI |
|-----------|--------|--------|--------------|
| Without BH mass | 0.600 | [0.609, 0.838] | Yes |
| With BH mass | 0.626 | [0.609, 0.835] | Yes |

**D-15 validation: PASS** — h=0.73 falls within 90% credible interval for both posteriors.

### Residual Bias
Posterior peaks at low h boundary (0.60-0.63) rather than at injected h=0.73. The simulation-based P_det corrects the shape relative to P_det=1 baseline (which peaked at 0.678) but does not fully resolve the bias. This is expected — other known systematics (wCDM params ignored, WMAP-era cosmology, galaxy redshift scaling) remain unfixed.
