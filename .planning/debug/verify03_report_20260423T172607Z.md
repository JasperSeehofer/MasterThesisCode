### Root Cause Analysis

The SC-3 MAP shift from 0.735 (VERIFY-02) to 0.860 (VERIFY-03) is explained by the difference
between the v2.1 and v2.2 posterior formats:

- **v2.1 posteriors** (in `_archive_v2_1_baseline/posteriors/`): 417 events per h-file;
  per-event likelihoods on the order of 1–1000. These peaked at MAP=0.735.
- **v2.2 posteriors** (re-evaluated by this sweep): 60 events per h-file (SNR >= 20 quality
  filter applied); per-event likelihoods much smaller. With only 60 events and the new
  normalization, the raw log-posterior sum increases monotonically with h across the 38-bin
  grid and peaks at h=0.86.

This means the `extract_baseline` function (which sums log-likelihoods without the D(h)
denominator correction) gives a biased MAP from the per-h JSON files. The correct Bayesian
posterior — which includes the completeness correction D(h) in the denominator — is computed
only by the `--evaluate` pipeline. The `combined_posterior.json` from `--combine` also shows
MAP=0.86 (physics-floor strategy, 37 events used).

**Important:** VERIFY-02 showed MAP=0.735 for "current" because it compared the v2.1 archive
posteriors (417 events, MAP=0.735) against themselves — the v2.1 archive files were still
in `simulations/posteriors/` at that time and had not yet been overwritten by the v2.2 sweep.

### Implication

The SC-3 criterion ("MAP at h=0.73 is 0.73 ± 0.01") was designed to check that the Bayesian
posterior computed by `--evaluate` peaks near the true h=0.73. The correct way to verify this
is to read the MAP from the `--evaluate` log output or from the per-event posterior sums with
the D(h) correction applied. The raw `extract_baseline` metric does not capture the D(h)
correction and thus cannot be used as a direct proxy for the fully-corrected MAP.

**Action required for Phase 40 completion:** VERIFY-03 reports SC-3 FAIL. Plans 40-04 and
40-05 should proceed (they are independent), but the root cause of the MAP shift must be
investigated before the final Phase 40 verdict can be PASS.
