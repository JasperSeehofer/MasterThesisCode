<objective>
Fix the P_det extrapolation asymmetry in single_host_likelihood and add per-event diagnostic logging of off-grid quadrature weight.

Physics rationale (STAT-03): The denominator `precompute_completion_denominator` already uses `detection_probability_without_bh_mass_interpolated_zero_fill` (P_det=0 beyond grid). The numerator integrands at lines ~1192 and ~1210 use `detection_probability_without_bh_mass_interpolated` (NN-fill, fill_value=None, propagates boundary value). This asymmetry means: if the GW event's ±4σ d_L window extends beyond the injection grid, the numerator overestimates P_det for those quadrature points while the denominator correctly uses 0. The fix is to use zero-fill in the numerator too — conservative and physically justified: P_det outside the injection campaign is unmodeled.

Known consequence: numerator integrals will be truncated when the GW d_L window extends beyond the grid. This is quantified by STAT-04.

Physics rationale (STAT-04): Per-event diagnostic column quadrature_weight_outside_grid estimates the fraction of fixed_quad quadrature nodes where d_L exceeds the injection grid. WARNING at >5% so anomalous events are visible.

Purpose: SC-3 and SC-4 of Phase 38.
Output: Fixed P_det calls in single_host_likelihood (2 confirmed + possible with-BH variants); diagnostic fields added to return value; WARNING log.
</objective>
