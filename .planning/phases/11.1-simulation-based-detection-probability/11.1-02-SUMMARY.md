## Decisions Made
- Used histogram2d binning instead of KDE for P_det grids: simpler, deterministic, no bandwidth tuning
- Geometric mean for log-spaced M bin centers preserves log-space uniformity
- Conservative zero fill for empty histogram bins (0/0 -> 0.0)
- Sky angles (phi, theta) accepted in API for compatibility but marginalized over internally per D-02
