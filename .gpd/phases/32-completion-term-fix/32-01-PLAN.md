<objective>
Implement the full-volume denominator D(h) for the completion term L_comp in the dark siren likelihood, per Gray et al. (2020) Eq. A.19.

Purpose: Fix the systematic low-h bias (MAP h=0.66-0.68 vs true h=0.73) caused by the current per-event local normalization of L_comp. The denominator should integrate P_det * dVc/dz over the full detectable volume, not just the local 4-sigma window.

Output: Modified `bayesian_statistics.py` with precomputed D(h) table, `simulation_detection_probability.py` with fill_value=0 P_det accessor, unit tests validating convergence and regression.
</objective>
